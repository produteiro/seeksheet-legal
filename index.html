This page is used to inform visitors regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use my Service.

If you choose to use my Service, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. However, SeekSheet is designed with strict privacy principles: it processes your data locally on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, the app requires access to certain device capabilities. The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission: Required to capture images of physical inventory sheets. The images are processed using Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract text.
  • Microphone Permission: Required to allow voice-activated searching within the scanned lists.

How your data is handled:

  • Local Processing: All image processing (OCR) and speech-to-text recognition happen locally on your device or through your device's built-in OS services.
  • No Cloud Storage: I do not collect, transmit, store, or upload your images, audio recordings, or extracted text to any external servers or third-party databases.
  • Local Data Persistence: The app saves your current scanning session (extracted text, bounding boxes, and list states) locally in the app's private internal storage. You can permanently delete this data at any time by using the "Clear All Sheets" feature within the app or by uninstalling the application.

2. Third-Party Services

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, strictly for providing core functionalities (like text recognition and speech recognition).

Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google ML Kit (Used for local on-device Text Recognition)

3. Security

I value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Because SeekSheet stores data locally and does not transmit data over the internet to developer servers, the risk of data breaches through the app is inherently minimized. Please ensure your device is secured with a password or biometrics to protect the local data.
